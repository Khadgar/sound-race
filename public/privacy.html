Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Sound Race is an offline, browser-based game. It does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no sign-ups, no cookies from third parties.

Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage (localStorage) solely to save:

  • Your chosen player name
  • Difficulty and graphics quality preferences
  • Per-track high scores

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time through your browser or app settings.

Audio Files

Audio files you load are processed entirely on your device for gameplay purposes. They are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Network Requests

The only network activity occurs when you choose to load an audio file from a URL you provide, or when featured tracks are downloaded for offline play on first launch. No other network requests are made.

Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected from anyone, no data is collected from children either.

Changes

If this policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
